While the heroes are very strong, they do experience weaknesses based on attack-type. When heroes are up against their weakness, the attack's damage is multipled by 1.5. Below is a breakdown of the attack-type weaknesses.

  • Strength is weak against Agility
  • Agility is weak against Mental
  • Mental is weak against Mechanical
  • Mechanical is weak against Elemental
  • Elemental is weak against Strength
