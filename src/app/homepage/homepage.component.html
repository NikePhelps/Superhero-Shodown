We find our heroes exhausted from saving the universe from certain destruction. Our Marvel heroes, fresh from defeating Thanos from altering reality, decide to attend an interdimensional bar to unwind before they go back to their homes. Upon arriving, they find the place a little crowded filled with other heroes who are unfamiliar. After banishing Darkseid to the Phantom Zone, our DC heroes decided to take a load off in the bar. Unbeknowst to our heroes, their battles have left their minds vulnerable. Brainiac has been tracking them and, sensing this, decides to inact his plan. Using powerful telepathy, he controls the heroes and pits them against each other with hopes of the Marvel heroes wiping out their DC counterparts. Will our heroes be able to overcome Braniac's power or will Braniac reign victorious? You will have to play to find out!
